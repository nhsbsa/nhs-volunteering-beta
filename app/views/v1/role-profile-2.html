Am I the right person for this?

This role will suit someone friendly, approachable and caring.

You don't need any previous experience. All you need is to:

  • have good conversational and listening skills
  • be comfortable approaching people to offer help
  • be comfortable approaching people to offer help

You don't need to have any qualifications or previous training.

What happens after your apply

Once you have submitted your application to volunteer with us, we will get in touch with you to discuss which roles are available and what you are best suited for.

About Leeds Teaching Hospitals NHS Trust

At Leeds Teaching Hospitals NHS Foundation Trust, we have a team of brilliant and valued volunteers who give their time to make a difference for patients and medical staff.

We have a dedicated team of staff who supports our volunteers from the start. We provide comprehensive induction and ongoing training. We can ensure you feel welcome and part of the team. We welcome applications from all sections of the community and are keen to diversify our volunteer workforce.
