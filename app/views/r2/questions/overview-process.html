Guidance

You need to include:

  • how and when you'll get in touch with the applicant
  • what happens after
  • a list of the checks they will have to go through
Example for a specific opportunity

After that, we will book in a chat to get to know you a bit more and walk you through the process.

Example for a generic opportunity

We welcome new volunteers at certain times during the year.

Examples of checks for all types of opportunities

There will be different types of checks required before you can start volunteering. These could include:

  • providing proof of identity and address
  • completing a criminal records check via the Disclosure and Barring Service (DBS) - this is free for volunteers
  • providing personal health and vaccination history information, and attending a health check
  • verifying that you are eligible to volunteer in the UK
  • providing details of your work and volunteering history
  • giving the details of some referees who we can contact in support of your application
