This will show on the opportunity listing under am I the right person for this?

Guidance

You need to include:

  • any required experience, qualification or training
  • the type of person you are looking for
  • any age limitations

Consider how you can make the service accessible to all

You should include if:

  • there is anything that might impact people with access needs such as walking or standing up for long hours
  • any required access to an electronic device and the level of skills needed

If you want to include your requirements for time commitment, you should:

  • show that you welcome everyone to volunteer
  • not use language that might discourage potential volunteers such as 'You need to volunteer for at least six months'
Example for all types of opportunities

This opportunity will suit someone friendly, approachable and enthusiastic.

You don't need any previous experience, qualifications or training. All you need is to:

  • have good people skills and want to chat with people
  • be reliable and be good at time keeping
  • enjoy the outdoors
  • be 16 or over
  • you need access to a telephone (for remote opportunities)

This opportunity requires some walking. If this doesn't suit you, we can discuss other ways you can volunteer with us.

Everyone is welcome to volunteer but we would encourage people to do it long-term.
