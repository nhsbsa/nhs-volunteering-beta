Privacy policy

This website is run by NHS Business Services Authority (NHSBSA) on behalf of NHS England and will be referred to as 'we' from now on.

As data processor, we are responsible for how we use, keep and share your information.

What information we collect

The personal data we collect from you includes:

  • your name and email address when you register for an opportunity
  • your postcode and address if you use the postcode or address lookup function on this service
  • questions, queries or feedback you leave, including your email address if you contact NHS Volunteering
  • your equality information, including your age range if you complete the optional questions when registering through NHS Volunteering
  • the details of which of which device and version of web browser you used
  • information on how you use the site, using cookies and page tagging techniques

Why we need your data

The law requires us to process your information so that the potential organisation you volunteer with can:

  • check you're eligible to volunteer
  • monitor the diversity of volunteers to ensure we comply with the Equality Act 2010

We collect your personal data so that we can:

  • personalise your visits to NHS Volunteering and improve the services we provide
  • provide customer service and support
  • communicate with you on a personal level
  • measure the performance of our service

What we do with your data

We may share your information with:

  • the NHS organisations and and Voluntary, community or social enterprise (VCSE) partner organisations you apply to volunteer with - they will hold copies of your application locally in their own systems as part of their registration process
  • NHS England and the Department of Health and Social Care for reporting purposes
  • any other organisation who has a legal right to it

We will not:

  • pass on your details to any third party or other government department for marketing purposes
  • transfer your personal data outside of the UK or the European Union (EU) (opens in a new tab)

How long we keep your data

We will only retain your personal data for as long as it is needed for the purposes set out in this document or for as long as the law requires us to.

We will:

  • keep your personal information for 24 months from the day you submit your registration
  • delete access log data after 24 months

How we protect your data and keep it secure

We are committed to doing all that we can to keep your data secure.  We have systems and processes in place to safeguard your data and keep strict security standards to prevent any unauthorised access to it.

Your rights

We manage the information you provide as required by Data Protection law. You have the right to:

  • receive a copy of the information we hold about you
  • request to change any information about you that is incorrect
  • request that your personal data is erased if there is no longer a justification for it

Links to other websites

NHS Volunteering contains links to other websites.

This privacy statement only covers the NHS Volunteering website. It does not cover other services and websites that it links to, so you should always be aware when you are moving to another site and read the privacy statement on that site.

Following a link to another website

If you go to another website from this one, read the privacy policy on that website to find out what it does with your information.

Following a link to NHS Volunteering from another website

If you come to NHS Volunteering from another website, we may receive personal information from the other website. You should read the privacy policy of the website you came from to find out more about this.
